# Sales & Customer Performance Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)
## Project Overview
This comprehensive Power BI project provides a deep-dive analysis of retail sales data, focusing on performance trends, profitability, customer behavior, and future forecasting. The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders make data-driven decisions by identifying growth opportunities and operational inefficiencies.

# Key Features & Dashboards

**Executive Sales Summary**
Overview KPIs: Instant visibility into Total Sales, Profit, Units Sold, and Customer count.

Performance Tracking: Top-selling products and most profitable items to identify core revenue drivers.

Discount Impact: Analysis of products with significant discounts and their correlation with sales volume.

**Time-Series & Trend Analysis**
Growth Monitoring: Year-over-Year (YOY) growth rates for orders and sales.

Seasonality: Identification of peak sales periods (Monthly/Quarterly) to optimize inventory and marketing.

Performance Trends: Comparison between MTD (Month-to-Date), QTD, and YTD metrics.

**Geographic Distribution**
Global Reach: Interactive map visualization showing order distribution across different countries and cities (covering 999 cities).

Regional Performance: Breakdown of sales and profit by geographic location to identify high-performing markets.

**Advanced Customer Intelligence**
Segmentation: Classification of customers into tiers (Bronze, Gold, Platinum, Diamond) based on purchase frequency and value.

Retention & Churn: Sophisticated analysis of New vs. Returning customers, including Retention Rates and Churn Rates over specific periods.

**Product & Cost Analysis**
Profitability Deep-Dive: Analysis of profit margins (12% overall) and unit cost vs. unit profit per product.

Category Performance: Distribution of orders across Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology.

Discount Analysis: Comparing sales performance and average profit for discounted vs. non-discounted orders.

What-If Analysis: Dynamic sliders to simulate the impact of changes in Sales and Profit growth rates.
## Tech Stack
Tool: Power BI Desktop

Data Modeling: Star Schema / Snowflake Schema.

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Used for advanced calculated measures (YOY Growth, Retention Rates, MTD/YTD).

Data Transformation: Power Query (M Language) for cleaning and reshaping data.

Visualizations: Map Integration, Time-Series Forecasting, Decomposition Trees, and Interactive Slicers.
## How to use
1-Download the .pbix file.

2-Open it using Power BI Desktop.

3- Use the Slicers on the top/side of each page to filter data by Category, Date, or Region.
